Cholera Bacteria

High turbidity + warm water = cholera risk. Causes severe diarrhea and dehydration.

Typhoid Bacteria

Low pH + high TDS environment. Causes typhoid fever with high mortality if untreated.

Gut Bacteria / Coliforms

Common in high-turbidity water. Causes diarrhea, vomiting, urinary infections.

🦠 Cholera
High turbidity + warm water → Cholera bacteria growth. Severe dehydration, can be fatal.
🌡️ Typhoid
Low pH + high TDS → Typhoid bacteria growth. High fever, weakness, abdominal pain.
💧 Diarrheal Disease
High turbidity + gut bacteria. Most common waterborne illness — vomiting, cramps.
🔬 Dysentery
High salinity + bacterial load → Shigella. Bloody diarrhea, fever, cramps.
