🏆 About AquaGuard — AquaGuard के बारे में

AquaGuard is a real-time IoT + AI water quality monitoring system developed by Alpha Coders, Government Engineering College Ajmer for Technova 2026. It is designed to support India's Jal Jeevan Mission by providing district health officials with actionable, real-time intelligence on rural water quality.

Use Case — Ajmer District: Rajasthan's groundwater shows fluoride contamination in 11+ districts. AquaGuard's IoT sensors provide continuous monitoring, while AI predicts disease outbreak risk before it happens — enabling proactive intervention by ASHA workers and district health officers.
