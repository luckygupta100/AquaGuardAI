📋 About This Portal

  • 💧
    Real-time Water Quality
    Monitor TDS, Turbidity, Temperature & Salinity of your village water supply from IoT sensors.
  • 🤖
    AI Disease Risk Alerts
    Groq AI engine predicts Cholera, Typhoid, Diarrhea risk based on live sensor data.
  • 📱
    ASHA Worker Reports
    Field workers submit daily health reports. Data flows to officials in real-time.
  • 📢
    SMS Alerts
    Automatic alerts sent to district health officer when contamination is detected.
